Features

What You Get

Credit Poa is more than a report. It is an ongoing financial wellness tool built around your real situation.

Your Loan Profile Report

A personalised PDF report covering your loan channel overview, risk positioning, borrowing activity assessment, key financial insights, repayment plan, and actionable recommendations. Generated the moment you submit your intake form. Downloadable anytime. Regenerated as many times as you need during an active subscription.

Loan Tracker

Log every active loan across all your apps. Track the amount borrowed, date taken, due date, and current status. Your loan tracker feeds your wellness score and repayment calendar automatically so everything stays in sync.

Repayment Calendar

A month view calendar that highlights every loan due date. Upcoming dues are shown in blue. Due today shows amber. Overdue shows red. Tap any date to see the loan details. In-app reminders notify you 3 days before a payment is due so you never go late again.

Financial Wellness Score

A score out of 100 calculated from your loan tracker data and intake form. Broken down into five dimensions: Repayment Consistency, Borrowing Volume, Balance Management, CRB Safety, and Limit Growth Potential. Each dimension shows a score and a one-line improvement tip. The number moves as your behaviour changes. You chase it upward.

Limit History

Log your loan limit on each app over time and watch it grow. A simple chart shows your progression across all apps. This is the feature that makes financial growth visible and motivating. Seeing the line go up is proof that your habits are working.

Repayment Plan and Goals

Set named financial goals and let the system track your progress. Clear Branch by August. Reduce active apps from 4 to 2. Qualify for KCB by December. Each goal has a completion percentage that updates as you log changes to your loans. You always know how far you have come and how far you have left to go.

About

About Credit Poa

An independent financial wellness platform built for Kenyan mobile loan users.

What Credit Poa Is

Credit Poa is a Kenyan financial wellness platform that provides individuals with a comprehensive review of their mobile lending activity. The platform equips users with the knowledge, tools, and strategic guidance to understand their borrowing behaviour, improve their loan profiles, and make informed financial decisions.

Credit Poa is not a lender, a credit bureau, or a financial institution. It is an independent information and advisory service positioned as a personal financial companion for Kenyans navigating the mobile lending landscape.

Who It Is For

Credit Poa is for anyone using mobile loan apps in Kenya who wants to understand their borrowing profile, improve their loan limits, prepare for a larger credit application, or simply build better financial habits. If you have ever wondered why your limits are not growing, or whether a lender would view you favourably, Credit Poa gives you the clarity to act with confidence.
